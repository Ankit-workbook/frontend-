Frequently Asked Questions

How does anonymity actually work?

Your account is created with just a username and password, with no connection to your real identity. We don't collect emails, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers. Additionally, our system automatically removes any potentially identifying information from posts.

Is there any moderation of content?

Yes, we have community guidelines to prevent harassment, hate speech, and illegal content. Our automated systems and community reporting help maintain a respectful environment. However, we do not filter or remove content simply because it presents a negative view of an institution.

How do I know the information is accurate?

College Reality operates on a community consensus model. Multiple independent reviews help establish patterns of truth. Users can upvote helpful or accurate content and downvote misleading information. We encourage readers to look for consistency across multiple posts rather than relying on any single review.

Can I delete my account or posts?

Yes, you have complete control over your content. You can delete your posts or entire account at any time through your profile settings. When you delete your account, all your posts and personal data are permanently removed from our system.

Can college administrators or staff see who I am?

Absolutely not. No one, including our own staff, can connect your username to your real identity. College administrators have no special access to user information and can only see what any other user can see on the platform.
